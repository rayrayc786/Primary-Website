OUR MISSION

Tech Entrepreneurship Networking

To create a thriving network of established businesses and, startups, students and anyone with ambition in the world of technology and entrepreneurship.

Financial Funding

To gather financial resources for the support and sustenance of startups, and for providing more opportunities to visionaries.

Mentorship Programs

To provide students with adequate guidance and knowledge from the best mentors, thus helping them prepare for their own startup journey.

Our Vision

Foster culture of entrepreneurship in the campus:

This initiative will instill a sense of direction and ambition in the youth, hence leading to a brighter future for the economy.

Inter University Collaboration:

It will bring in diverse perspectives and ideologies, creating a more widespread creative safe space for new projects.

To Become the Best Incubation Platform:

Catering to the needs of flourishing startups with immense potential and providing them with necessary resources, this will act as a catalyst for the rapid growth of the economy.

ABOUT GNDU

Guru Nanak Dev University was established at Amritsar, Punjab, India on 24 November 1969 to commemorate Guru Nanak Dev's birth quincentenary. Guru Nanak Dev University's campus is spread over 500 acres near village of Kot Khalsa, nearly 8 km west of the Amritsar, next to Khalsa College, Amritsar.
